How to Play Dobble

Dobble is a fast-paced visual observation game for 2–8 players, ages 7 and up. A round lasts about 15 minutes.

The Golden Rule

Any two cards in the deck share exactly one matching symbol — same shape and color, but possibly a different size. This is always true, for every pair.

Five Mini-Games

All five mini-games share the same core mechanic — find the one matching symbol between two cards and call it out. What changes between games is the objective and how cards are won or lost.

1

The Tower

Goal: Collect the most cards.

  1. Deal one card face-down to each player. Stack the rest face-up in the center.
  2. Everyone flips their card at the same time.
  3. Race to spot the symbol shared between your card and the top of the center pile.
  4. Call it out — take that center card and place it on top of yours.
  5. All players now compare their new top card against the next center card.
  6. When the center pile runs out, the player with the most cards wins.
2

The Well

Goal: Be the first to discard all your cards.

  1. Stack all cards face-up in the center. Deal 5 cards face-down to each player.
  2. Everyone races to match their top card against the center pile's top card.
  3. Call out the match — discard your card onto the center pile.
  4. First player to empty their hand wins.
3

Poisoned Gift

Goal: End up with the fewest cards.

  1. Deal all cards face-down to players. Flip one to start a central discard pile.
  2. Find the symbol that matches between another player's top card and the center card.
  3. Call it out — pass your top card to that player as an unwanted "gift".
  4. When all cards are distributed, the player with the fewest cards wins.
4

Catch Them All

Goal: Collect the most cards from the table.

  1. Spread several cards face-up in a ring around a draw pile in the center.
  2. Each player holds one card and races to match it against any card in the ring.
  3. Call out the match — claim that ring card and place it on your pile.
  4. Flip a new card from the draw pile to refill the ring.
  5. Most cards collected at the end wins.
5

Hot Potato

Goal: Hold the fewest cards when the pile runs out.

  1. Deal one card to each player. Stack the rest face-up in the center.
  2. Find the symbol shared between your card and the top of the center pile.
  3. Call it out — pass the center card to any opponent of your choice.
  4. That player now holds two cards and must deal with both.
  5. Fewest cards in hand when the pile is empty wins.
